The Problem

The mis-diagnosis of autoimmune diseases creates a significant amount of healthcare waste and impacts patients' ability to manage their condition effectively

High Prevalence of Autoimmune Disease

There are over 80 autoimmune diseases. Approximately one in six Americans has an autoimmune diseases, but only half of these have a diagnosis.

Diagnosis
is difficult

It takes 4.5 years on average to receive a correct AD diagnosis as approximately 40% of patients are initially mis-diagnosed.

Delayed diagnosis results in high associated healthcare costs

On average hospitalization costs were 1.9% higher with delay (p=0.04).

SOLUTIONS

  • 1.Medical Data Collections

    AD.AI colloborates with hospitals and pharmacies to get patients' deidentified data and with the combined exhaustive data, AD.AI leverages machine learning to aid pharmacist and specialist to diagnoze diseases.

  • 2. Life Environment Survelliance

    AD.AI will also integrate data from wearble devices and food record apps to know the patients' daily activities and diet, so any patients' abnormality will be detectd at a faster speed.

  • 3. Treatment Help

    After AD.AI gives the basic diagonis, patients will be referred to certain specialists with a focus on patients' relevant diseases and get right treatment.
